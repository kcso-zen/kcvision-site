Why Do Privacy Coins Matter?為什麼隱私幣重要？

When all financial activity is monitored, freedom has already vanished. Monero, Zcash and other privacy coins give users genuine anonymity and sovereignty.當所有人的財務活動都被監控，自由就已消失。Monero、Zcash 等隱私幣，讓交易者真正擁有匿名性與自主權。

  • · On-chain privacy: Zero-knowledge proofs ZK-SNARKs鏈上隱私技術：零知識證明 ZK-SNARKs
  • · Ring signatures and stealth addresses環形簽名與隱蔽地址
  • · Tornado Cash incident and censorship resistanceTornado Cash 事件與審查抵抗

KC's Privacy ConvictionKC 的隱私信念

Privacy is not equal to crime. Choosing financial privacy is a rational resistance to surveillance capitalism. Everyone should have the right to decide who can see their assets.隱私不等於犯罪。選擇財務隱私，是對監控資本主義的理性抵抗。每個人都應該有權決定誰能查看自己的資產。

⚠️ 「When privacy becomes a crime, only criminals have privacy.當隱私變成罪行，只有罪犯才有隱私。」
— Eric Hughes, Cypherpunk Manifesto
PRACTICAL GUIDE實戰指南

Privacy Protection Playbook隱私保護實戰手冊

Ideas need to be grounded in action. Here is KC's personally-tested privacy toolkit, from beginner to advanced.理念要落地，隱私保護需要具體行動。這是 KC 親身實踐的隱私工具清單，從入門到進階。

01

Hardware Wallet First硬件錢包優先

Ledger / Trezor offline key storage. Never store your seed phrase in the cloud or as a screenshot.Ledger / Trezor 離線存儲私鑰，永遠不要將助記詞存放在雲端或截圖保存。

02

Use Decentralised Exchanges使用去中心化交易所

Uniswap, dYdX — no KYC, reducing personal data exposure risk.Uniswap、dYdX 無需 KYC，減少個人數據洩露風險。

03

Anonymous Tools匿名工具配置

Tor Browser + VPN dual protection. Works best combined with privacy coins.Tor Browser + VPN 雙重保護，配合隱私幣使用效果最佳。

04

Self-Sovereign Identity自我主權身份

DID and ENS domains — building digital identity that doesn't depend on any platform.DID（去中心化身份）與 ENS 域名，建立不依賴平台的數字身份。
