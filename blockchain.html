Blockchain: Redefining
Trust at the Base Layer區塊鏈：
重新定義信任的底層革命

Blockchain is not just the technical foundation of Bitcoin — it is a fundamental upgrade to how humanity collaborates. Three core pillars hold up the roof of the decentralised world.區塊鏈不僅是比特幣的技術基礎，更是人類協作方式的根本性升級。三大核心支柱，撐起去中心化世界的穹頂。

👁️

Transparency透明性

All transactions publicly verifiable by anyone, without needing a central authority's endorsement. Code is law, rules are consensus.所有交易公開可查，任何人可驗證，無需依賴中央機構背書。代碼即法律，規則即共識。

🔐

Immutability不可篡改

Each block cryptographically links to the previous one. Altering history requires over 51% of total network hash power — practically impossible.每個區塊以密碼學連結前一區塊，修改歷史需要超過全網 51% 算力，在實踐中幾乎不可能。

🌐

Decentralisation去中心化

No single point of failure, no single controller. Thousands of nodes scattered globally — no government or company can shut this network down alone.無單點故障、無單一控制者。數千個節點分散全球，任何政府或企業都無法單獨關閉這個網絡。
