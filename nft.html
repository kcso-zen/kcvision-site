NFT: Beyond the Hype,
Redefining OwnershipNFT：超越炒作，
重新定義所有權

NFTs are not JPEGs — they are blockchain-issued, unforgeable proof of ownership for digital assets. After the bubble fades, real value emerges.NFT 的本質不是 JPEG，而是區塊鏈賦予數字資產的不可偽造所有權證明。泡沫褪去後，真正的價值才開始顯現。

Creator Economy創作者經濟

Artists trade directly with collectors for the first time — no gallery cuts, no label fees. Royalties auto-execute on-chain forever.藝術家首次能直接與收藏者交易，無需畫廊或唱片公司抽成，版稅自動化永久執行。

Gaming Assets遊戲資產

Players truly own in-game items, transferable across platforms. Virtual land and gear become real assets.玩家真正擁有遊戲內物品，可跨平台轉移交易。虛擬土地、道具成為真實資產。

Digital Identity數字身份

ENS domains and Soulbound Tokens (SBT) — a new paradigm of decentralised identity owned by you alone.ENS 域名、靈魂綁定代幣（SBT）開創去中心化數字身份新範式，你的身份由你掌控。

Music & Royalties音樂與版權

Music NFTs let artists sell royalty shares directly to fans — fans become beneficiaries.音樂 NFT 讓歌手直接向粉絲出售作品版權份額，粉絲也能成為版稅受益人。
