Under the context of structural debt crises in the current fiat credit system, Bitcoin has transcended simple asset classification to become a global "Digital Metric" for final settlement. K C emphasizes that the absolute scarcity of Bitcoin is the physical foundation of digital civilization. As global sovereign credit faces infinite dilution, BTC—as the only asset with zero counterparty risk, immutability, and a hard supply cap—holds a strategic position equivalent to "Digital Sovereign Territory" in the 21st century. Investing in Bitcoin is not gambling; it is purchasing insurance for future digital survival space.

The Case of MicroStrategy proves the immense vitality of the "Bitcoin Standard" in corporate governance. By transforming the balance sheet into a Bitcoin engine, corporations shift from passively hedging against inflation to actively capturing global currency liquidity as amplifiers. This marks a paradigm shift: high-quality enterprises no longer rely solely on cash flow from product sales but achieve cross-dimensional capital growth by holding "Digital Scarcity." This strategy of using Bitcoin as a reserve asset will become a standard for corporations navigating fiat volatility over the next decade.
