1Is Bitcoin still worth buying?比特幣還值得買嗎？

From a long-term perspective, Bitcoin is still in the early stages of global adoption. Institutional entry and ETF approval continue to strengthen the fundamentals.以長期視角看，比特幣仍處於全球採用早期。機構入場、ETF 獲批，基本面持續強化。

2Is crypto safe?加密貨幣安全嗎？

The technology itself is safe, but human error is the biggest risk. Hardware wallet + correct backup = your assets in your own hands.技術本身安全，但人為錯誤是最大風險。硬件錢包 + 正確備份，資產安全掌握在自己手中。

3Does NFT still have a future?NFT 還有未來嗎？

The speculative bubble has burst, but the digital ownership infrastructure is still being built. NFT projects with genuine use cases retain long-term value.投機泡沫已破，但數字所有權基礎設施仍在建設中。真正有用例的 NFT 項目長期仍有價值。

4Is DeFi safe?DeFi 安全嗎？

Smart contract risk is real. Only use audited protocols, start small, and never go all-in on a single platform.智能合約風險真實存在。只使用經審計的協議，小倉位試水，永遠不要 all-in 單一平台。

5How do I start learning about blockchain?如何開始學習區塊鏈？

Start with the Bitcoin white paper, then Ethereum documentation. Hands-on experience beats any tutorial.從比特幣白皮書開始，然後是以太坊文檔。親身操作比任何教程都更有效。
